About this page

This page is designed to be a simple guide to when the major conventions are in Ireland.

Basic information about where the event takes place will be in the timeline.

The next event will have a greater focus, including specifics on locations if that convention is in an awkward to get to place or a particular building on a college campus, for example.

This is all with you in mind to make planning easier, and to give every convention possible the attention it deserves.

Note:

Dates are listed in the standard European format of Day / Month.

In the occurence that there is more than one event on a particular day, both will feature in alphabetical order. No preferences will be given to events based on size.

Running an event but don't see it listed? Head on over to our contact page and we'll be delighted to add you to the list.
